Shield of Chivalry
A metal shield, bound in brown suede, bearing a badge of a blue tyger combattant.

"Shield of Chivalry (new tradition, a shield (gift from Atenveldt) to be bestowed upon the combatant the Queen (in consultation with the Ladies of the Rose) considers the most chivalrous in the Crown Tourney)" -- Sedalia Regina, 1983
