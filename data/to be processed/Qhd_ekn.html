Queen’s Honor of Distinction

 

The QHD is an honor instituted by Edict of HRM Elspeth. It is intended to recognize worthy deeds and good works not otherwise covered by the OBT, KOE, QOC, Cyphers, etc. It is given entirely at the Queen’s discretion, for such reasons as she sees fit. The token bears her cognizance (a daisy), and is crafted by her own hand; it is similar to the Neddingham Daisy (a personal favor of HRM as Baroness Bridge) but is larger. No scrolls are given for this award.

The award will presumably be closed at the end of Elspeth’s reign.
