Awards in the reign of Jehan and Lauryan

October  15, 1972 – January 6, 1973

6 Jan 73 – Twelfth Night*

Order of the Golden Dragon dissolved. Its two members, El and Aravis, henceforth members of the Order of the Silver Crescent.

Additionally:

Alfgar the Sententious – Silver Crescent

Asbjorn Gustafsson – Knight

Akbar and Khadijah crowned as Shah and Shahanou, for the third time

* The awards presented at this event may have been from either Jehan and Lauryan, or Akbar III and Khadijah III, records are unclear.
