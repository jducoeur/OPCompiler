This is the Order of Precedence and Rolls of Orders of the East Kingdom of the SCA.  It is maintained by Mistress Caitlin Davies, Shepherd's Crook Herald (Jane Waks, waks@world.std.com or waks@mediaone.net).

The records in these files are current to the best of my ability and knowledge. If you find a mistake or omission, please let me know. (If you are looking for court data that is less than a month or two old, please be lenient. I tend to enter data in batches.)

For a more complete description of each of the orders, see Awards and Honors in the East Kingdom, by Baroness Jessa d'Avondale OP, or Kingdom Law.  The next printed edition of the Order of Precedence will be issued in Spring 1999.  In case of a discrepancy between these online files and the printed text, the printed text shall be considered authoritative.

This site created 20 June 96, last updated 21 March 1999. East Kingdom data are correct through 20 February, King & Queen's Fencing Championships (except for a few corrections I haven't typed in yet [hi Dupont!]).  Files and orders not yet marked as links are coming Real Soon Now.
