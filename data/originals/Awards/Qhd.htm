Queen’s Honor of Distinction

The QHD is an honor instituted by Edict of HRM Elspeth, and continued by various subsequent Queens of the East.  It is intended to recognize worthy deeds and good works not otherwise covered by the OBT, KOE, QOC, Cyphers, etc.  It is given entirely at the Queen’s discretion, for such reasons as she sees fit.  The token bears her cognizance or other symbol of the Queen’s choice.  No scrolls are given for this award.
