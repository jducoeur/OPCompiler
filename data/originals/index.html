This is the Order of Precedence and Rolls of Orders of the East Kingdom of the SCA.  It is maintained by Duchess Anna Ophelia Holloway, Shepherd's Crook Herald (shepherds.crook@eastkingdom.org )

The records in these files are current to the best of my ability and knowledge. If you find a mistake or omission, please let me know. (If you are looking for court data that is less than a month or so old, please be lenient.  I tend to enter data in batches.)  This site exists in multiple locations including http://www.waks.org/Prec/  and http://op.eastkingdom.org/  If you have difficulty using one of them, please try another.  The authoritative versions of the files are held by the Shepherd's Crook Herald.

Printed editions of the Order of Precedence are available by mail from me for $5 including postage (make checks payable to SCA, Inc., East Kingdom).  Canadian and overseas addresses please enquire for pricing.

For a more complete description of each of the orders, see Awards and Honors in the East Kingdom, by Baroness Jessa d'Avondale OP, or Kingdom Law.
