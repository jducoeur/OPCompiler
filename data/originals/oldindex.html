Order of Precedence of the East Kingdom

This is the Order of Precedence and Rolls of Orders of the East Kingdom of the SCA. It is maintained by Mistress Caitlin Davies, Shepherd's Crook Herald (Jane Waks, waks@world.std.com or waks@mediaone.net).

The records in these files are current to the best of my ability and knowledge. If you find a mistake or omission, please let me know. (If you are looking for court data that is less than a month or two old, please be lenient. I tend to enter data in batches.)

For a more complete description of each of the orders, see Awards and Honors in the East Kingdom, by Baroness Jessa d'Avondale OP, or Kingdom Law. The next printed edition of the Order of Precedence will be issued Really Soon Now (I had planned on having it out this past winter). In case of a discrepancy between these online files and the printed text, the printed text shall be considered authoritative.

This file created 20 June 96, last modified 8 July 98. East Kingdom data are correct through 4 July. AEthelmearc data are current through its last principality court on 20 September. Files and orders not yet marked as links are coming Real Soon Now.
